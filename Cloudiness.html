Cloudiness

Clouds appear to be fairly evenly distributed regardless of latitude, with large numbers of datapoints at 0 (no clouds) or in bands at 20%, 40$, 75% or 90%. This could be due to the scale cloudiness is measured. Otherwise, there appears to be no relation between cloudiness and latitude.
